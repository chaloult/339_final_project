Teenage Years Video Games

Below are some of the most memorable video games from my teenage years.

Click on a card to flip it over and reveal the game!

STAGE 1 Pokemon Moon HP 90

Pokemon Moon

This was the first Pokemon game that I ever played, and started my love for the series. I loved the game so much I finished it in one week!

Console: Nintendo 3DS

♡♡♡

STAGE 1 Stardew Valley HP 90

Stardew Valley

I remember being obsessed with this game the summer before I started 9th grade, and probably spent over 100 hours on it that summer alone!

Console: PC

♡♡♡

STAGE 1 Animal Crossing: NL HP 90

Animal Crossing: New Leaf

Probably my most played game from the Animal Crossing series. I have over 200 hours logged on my 3DS!

Console: Nintendo 3DS

♡♡♡

STAGE 1 Ace Attorney HP 90

Ace Attorney Trilogy

This might've been the first ever visual novel game that I ever played. I don't remember how I discovered the game, but with how much I adore the series I am glad I did!

Console: Nintendo 3DS

♡♡♡

STAGE 1 Genshin Impact HP 90

Genshin Impact

I'm glad that I can't see how many hours I have on this game, because it pretty much consumed me for most of 10th grade.

Console: PC

♡♡♡

STAGE 1 BanG Dream! HP 90

BanG Dream! Girls Band Party!

Though not my first rhythm game, finding this game reignited my love for the genre. I may not play the game anymore, but I still listen to the amazing music from it.

Console: Mobile
